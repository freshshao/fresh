abstract: >-
  Guiding vectors can direct evolutionary algorithms to approximate sparse Pareto optimal solutions. This paper proposes a deep reinforcement learning assisted guiding vector selection method (AGSEA). By regarding population features as states, performance improvements as rewards, and candidate guiding vectors as actions, an agent uses deep neural networks to establish the mapping model and automatically determine the best guiding vector according to the current population state.
