abstract: >-
  This paper proposes a knowledge learning-based dimensionality reduction approach for large-scale sparse multiobjective optimization. By extracting knowledge from previously found solutions, the method effectively reduces the search space dimensionality, enabling evolutionary algorithms to focus on promising regions and identify sparse Pareto optimal solutions more efficiently.
